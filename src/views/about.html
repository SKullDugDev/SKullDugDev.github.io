William William Edgar Wright is a modern day American poet based in Maryland.

William was penned out of a necessity to write and create. He was formed as a catalyst to extract beauty and insight from the mind, endless ocean muddied and mucked slow. Stranded and stuck searching for an outlet, Wright came to be in those short breaths of sanity that could be called clarity. And in those moments when words came, a song waiting to be sung, he etched them down and had them bound in an abstract page. Over and over again this ritual was repeated until the voice that is William Edgar Wright truly came to fruition with a distinct identity.

He is an attempt to understand via the lens of poetry, to tell a story that resonates on a level basic to the human condition. There is a beauty and comfort to the written word that exists both in the mind and aloud once given breath. In an absurdist embrace of the futility of "the right word", William chooses to take you along on his journey of self-discovery in the hopes that the paths taken and the worlds described might accomplish something. What that is, not even he knows.

His career began in 2018 with his debut collection, ".a period in time". The collection is a catalogue of the emotions and experiences formed over the span of almost two years. Some of his work sheds light on darker and more difficult life experiences such as the struggles of mental health as well as life and relationships. He has also written insights on the world and natural beauty. The eclectic nature of the themes discussed is a reflection of the eclectic nature of the human mind, specifically Edgar's, given form through the crucible of poetic structure.

Come along and dive into an exploration of the subjective nature of objective truth and test the limits of language in its ability to bring together and describe what may or may not be quite there.
